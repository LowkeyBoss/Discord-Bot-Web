Frequently Asked Questions

What moderation commands does Sonic offer?
Sonic includes commands for kicking, banning, timing out members, purging messages, and more. It also supports logging mod actions for transparency.
How does the anonymous confession system work?
Members can send anonymous confessions with optional images and replies. Moderators can manage user permissions and view logs to ensure safe use.
Is Sonic free to use?
Yes! Sonic is completely free to invite and use in your server.
How often is Sonic updated?
Sonic is in open beta and receives constant updates based on community feedback to improve features and add new ones.
Where can I get support or join the community?
Join our Discord server for support, announcements, and to chat with the community: https://discord.gg/X49uQx7dMD
