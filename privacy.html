Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Sonic Bot collects minimal data necessary to operate and improve its functionality, such as Discord user IDs and server IDs to provide moderation and feature services.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to power commands, provide stats, and maintain moderation features. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely with restricted access. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.

Your Choices

You can remove Sonic Bot from your server at any time to stop data collection. For any questions or concerns, please contact us through our support channels.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes. We encourage you to review it periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
