Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite Sonic to my server?

Use the official invite link to add Sonic with the recommended permissions.

What moderation commands does Sonic offer?

Sonic supports kick, ban, timeout, purge, and mod action logging. For full details, visit the Commands page.

How can I report bugs or suggest features?

Join the community server and share your feedback in the #suggestions channel.

Why do some commands require administrator permissions?

Certain moderation actions need higher permissions to ensure your server’s security and prevent misuse.
